Pickles

The best homemade pickles

Let's make these amazing pickles together!

(All photos and texts taken from Cookie and Kate)

Ingredients

  • vinegar (I prefer rice vinegar's mild flavor)
  • fresh dill (technically optional, but delicious)
  • a couple cloves of garlic
  • a few basic seasonings

General steps

  1. Slice your cucumbers as desired.
  2. Whisk together a basic brine made of water, vinegar and seasonings.
  3. Pack the cucumbers into a jar, add some dill and garlic, and pour the brine over it all.
  4. Refrigerate until the pickles taste sufficiently “pickled!”
